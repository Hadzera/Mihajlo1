Besides his patents he published several dozen scientific disputes, articles, reviews and a 396-page autobiography under the name Michael Pupin, From Immigrant to Inventor (Scribner's, 1923).[24] He won the annual Pulitzer Prize for Biography or Autobiography.[25][26] It was published in Serbian in 1929 under the title From pastures to scientist (Od pašnjaka do naučenjaka).
